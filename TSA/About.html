About Skyline TSA

Our Chapter

We are Skyline High School of TSA, Technology Student Association. We are comprised of some of the brightest students in Skyline who have devoted themselves to the study and advancement of science, technology, engineering and math (STEM). This year, Skyline TSA is a brand new extra-curricular educational club in Skyline that students have access to. This year we have a total of 26 members and this number will only continue to grow as TSA grows over the coming years.

Meetings

Our chapter meets every week on Wednesdays and Thursdays after school from 2:30 - 3:30 pm. Meetings are held in Mr. Kapptie's classroom, room 303. Members can attend meetings on either day, as TSA is structured to be flexible around its members who may be busy with other activities such as sports or other extra-curriculars.

How To Join

If you are interested in joining Skyline TSA you can contact either Mr. Kapptie or any one of the presidency members on how to join. Otherwise you can just go to any TSA meeting and ask to join. The fee to join TSA is $25.00.
